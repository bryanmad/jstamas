Logistics

Players will meet in the lounge before tee off. If you want a Sunday lunch, please ensure this is ordered 1 hour before your tee off time.

Please make your way to the tee in advance of your tee time and tee off after the group ahead have finished their approach shots to the green.

There will be a short presentation as soon as the last group finishes.
